Your Security

Your money and personal information are protected by multiple layers of bank-grade security and industry-leading safeguards.

CouplesCash uses the same security standards as major banks and financial institutions. Your funds are FDIC insured and your data is encrypted with military-grade protection.

FDIC Insurance Protection

Your money is safe and protected through our banking partner, Evolve Bank & Trust, Member FDIC.

1

Individual Coverage: $250,000

Each partner's portion of the joint account is insured up to $250,000 by the FDIC.

2

Joint Account Coverage: $500,000

As a joint account, your total coverage is $500,000 per couple - double the individual limit.

3

Automatic Protection

FDIC insurance is automatic and free. No additional fees or applications required.

What FDIC insurance covers: Your deposits are protected even if Evolve Bank & Trust were to fail. The FDIC would either transfer your account to another bank or pay you directly, up to the insured amount.

How You Can Stay Secure

Security Best Practices

  • Strong Password: Use a unique, complex password just for CouplesCash
  • Enable Biometrics: Use fingerprint or Face ID when available
  • Keep Apps Updated: Install updates promptly for security patches
  • Secure WiFi Only: Avoid banking on public WiFi networks
  • Check Statements: Review transactions regularly for unauthorized activity
  • Report Issues Fast: Contact us immediately if something seems wrong
  • Log Out When Done: Don't stay logged in on shared devices
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something feels suspicious, it probably is
